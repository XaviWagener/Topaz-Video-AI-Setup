Upscaling increases the resolution of your videos.

1. Drag your video file into the project window.

3. Choose an AI model suited for your footage type.

### How to stabilize footage

Stabilization removes camera shake from handheld shots.

1. Import your shaky video file.
2. Select the stabilization filter from the list of effects.
3. Choose the strength of the correction.
4. Press Preview to see the changes before you save the file.
5. Use the Export button to save the final video to your disk.

### How to use frame interpolation

Interpolation adds missing frames to your video. This makes movement appear fluid.

1. Load a video with a low frame rate.
2. Go to the motion settings tab.
3. Enable frame interpolation.
4. Set the target frame rate, such as 60 frames per second.
5. Initiate the export process.

## 📡 Frequently asked questions

### Will the software change my source files?
No. The application creates a new file. Your original video remains unchanged on your storage drive.

### Why does processing take a long time?
The software uses complex math to enhance each frame. High-resolution videos require more time. Ensure you have a powerful graphics card to speed up these tasks.

### Can I cancel a process?
Yes. Click the stop button in the lower corner of the window. The software saves the progress it made until the moment you stopped it.

### Do I need an internet connection?
You need an internet connection to download the installer and verify the software license. Most AI models download the first time you select them. Once the models exist on your computer, you can work offline.

### What should I do if the software crashes?
Close the application and restart your computer. If the problem persists, check for graphics driver updates from your hardware manufacturer website. 

## 🛡️ Best practices for video quality

- Use original high-quality footage for the best results.
- Do not upscale a video more than four times its original resolution. 
- Use the preview window to test different models before you commit to a long export.
- Keep your computer cool while the software processes files. Heavy use of your graphics card generates heat.
- Save your output files in a format like MP4 for better compatibility with other programs.
